# Face Recognition

The system uses facial recognition to verify employee identity at every check-in and check-out. This ensures that attendance records are tied to the correct individual and prevents proxy attendance (one employee checking in on behalf of another).

---

## How It Works

When an employee checks in, the system:

1. Captures a frame from the device camera.
2. Detects the face within the frame.
3. Generates a **facial embedding** — a numerical vector representing the unique features of the face.
4. Compares the embedding against the employee's enrolled profile.
5. If the similarity score meets or exceeds the match threshold, verification passes.

The entire process completes in under 3 seconds under normal conditions.

---

## Enrollment

Face enrollment registers your facial profile in the system. It is completed once during onboarding and is required before your first check-in.

### Enrollment Steps
1. From the dashboard, click **Complete Face Enrollment**.
2. Allow camera access when prompted.
3. Position your face within the on-screen frame.
4. The system captures multiple frames from slightly different angles to build a robust profile.
5. A progress indicator fills as frames are captured — do not move until it reaches 100%.
6. On success, the system displays **Enrollment Successful** and activates check-in.

### Photo Requirements During Enrollment

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| Lighting | Even, front-facing light. Avoid backlighting or harsh shadows. |
| Expression | Neutral expression preferred; slight variation is acceptable. |
| Obstructions | No sunglasses, masks, or hats that cover facial features. |
| Distance | Face should fill approximately 60–80% of the frame. |
| Movement | Stay still during capture. |

### Enrollment Failure
If enrollment fails:
- Re-check lighting and camera positioning.
- Retry up to 3 times.
- After 3 consecutive failures, the system locks enrollment and generates an alert for the administrator. Contact your admin to reset the enrollment and try again.

---

## Verification at Check-In

At each check-in or check-out, the system runs verification against the stored enrollment profile.

### Verification Outcome

| Result | Condition | Action Taken |
|---|---|---|
| **Pass** | Similarity score ≥ match threshold | Check-in recorded |
| **Fail — Low Confidence** | Similarity score below threshold | Retry allowed (up to 3 attempts) |
| **Fail — No Face Detected** | No face found in the frame | Retry with repositioning prompt |
| **Fail — Multiple Faces** | More than one face detected | Retry in a private area |

After 3 consecutive verification failures at check-in, the system logs an anomaly alert and the employee is prompted to contact their manager or administrator.

---

## Re-Enrollment Triggers

An employee may need to re-enroll in the following scenarios:

| Trigger | Reason |
|---|---|
| Significant appearance change | New facial hair, hairstyle change, or post-surgery recovery affecting facial structure |
| Consistent verification failures | Repeated match failures despite good lighting and positioning |
| Administrator reset | Admin manually resets enrollment as part of troubleshooting |

To initiate re-enrollment, go to **Profile → Settings → Face Re-Enrollment** and follow the same steps as initial enrollment.

---

## Fallback to Manual Check-In

If face verification is unavailable due to a camera failure or device permission issue, the system falls back to **manual check-in**:

1. The employee notifies their manager.
2. The manager logs the attendance manually via the Manager Dashboard → **Manual Attendance Entry**.
3. The manual entry is flagged in the attendance record and included in audit logs.

> Manual check-ins do not generate a face verification result. They are tracked separately in reports.

## Privacy and Data Handling

| Detail | Policy |
|---|---|
| **What is stored** | Facial embeddings (numerical vectors), not raw photos |
| **Where stored** | Encrypted in Cloud SQL (PostgreSQL), access restricted to the ML inference service |
| **Retention** | Embeddings are deleted within 30 days of account deactivation |
| **Access** | No human reviewer has access to raw embeddings; they are only used by the inference model |
| **Raw images** | Frames captured during enrollment and verification are not stored permanently |

For full data handling policy, see [Data Privacy and Security](../reference/data-privacy-and-security.md).
