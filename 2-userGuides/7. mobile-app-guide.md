# Mobile App Guide

This guide covers installation, permission setup, and all mobile-specific features of the Attendance Tracking app — built with React Native (Expo) for Android and iOS.

---

## Installation

### Android
1. Open the **Google Play Store**.
2. Search for **"Attendance Tracker — [Your Organization Name]"**.
3. Tap **Install**. The app requires approximately 150 MB of storage.
4. Once installed, tap **Open**.

### iOS
1. Open the **App Store**.
2. Search for **"Attendance Tracker — [Your Organization Name]"**.
3. Tap **Get** and authenticate with Face ID / Touch ID or your Apple ID password.
4. Once installed, tap **Open**.

> If the app is distributed internally (not via public app stores), your IT administrator will provide a direct installation link or MDM-pushed install.

---

## Granting Required Permissions

On first launch, the app requests the following permissions. All three are required for full functionality.

### Camera
- **Purpose:** Face verification during check-in and check-out.
- **When prompted:** Tap **Allow**.
- **If denied:** Go to your device Settings → Apps → Attendance Tracker → Permissions → Camera → Allow.

### Location (Precise)
- **Purpose:** GPS-based geofence validation at check-in.
- **When prompted:** Select **Allow While Using the App** (Android) or **Allow Once / Always** (iOS — select **While Using**).
- **If denied:** Go to device Settings → Privacy → Location Services → Attendance Tracker → While Using.

> **Important:** Select **Precise Location** when given the option. Approximate location is not sufficient for geofence validation.

### Notifications
- **Purpose:** Attendance reminders, anomaly alerts, and system announcements.
- **When prompted:** Tap **Allow**.
- **If denied:** Go to device Settings → Notifications → Attendance Tracker → Enable.

## Login and Biometric Session Setup

1. Open the app and enter your **email** and **password**.
2. After successful login, the app will offer to enable **biometric authentication** (Face ID on iOS, Fingerprint or Face Unlock on Android).
   - Tap **Enable** to allow future logins without typing your password.
   - Tap **Skip** to continue using password login only.
3. Your session remains active for 8 hours. After that, you will be prompted to re-authenticate.

---

## GPS-Verified Check-In

1. Tap **Check In** on the home screen.
2. The app requests your current GPS location. Ensure Location permission is set to **While Using**.
3. The system validates your position against the configured geofence zone.
   - **Green indicator:** Location verified — you are within the approved zone.
   - **Red indicator:** Outside zone — check-in will not proceed. See [GIS Location Validation](../features/gis-location-validation.md) for details.
4. Once location is verified, face verification begins automatically (Step 5 below).

> GPS lock can take 5–15 seconds, especially when first opening the app indoors. If the location indicator spins for more than 20 seconds, move closer to a window and retry.

---

## Face Verification at Check-In

1. The camera opens automatically after GPS verification.
2. Center your face within the on-screen oval frame.
3. Hold still — the system captures and matches your face against your enrolled profile.
4. **Verification Successful:** Check-in is recorded and a timestamp confirmation is displayed.
5. **Verification Failed:** You will see an error and can retry up to 3 times. After 3 failures, the system logs an alert and prompts you to contact your administrator.

### Tips for Reliable Verification
- Ensure the front-facing camera lens is clean.
- Check in a well-lit area — avoid strong light sources directly behind you.
- Look directly at the camera; tilting your head at a steep angle may cause failure.

---

## Check-Out

1. Tap **Check Out** on the home screen.
2. Face verification runs once.
3. Your work hours for the day are displayed immediately after confirmation.

> If you forget to check out, the system will flag the session as incomplete. Contact your manager or HR to correct the record manually.

## Viewing Your Dashboard on Mobile

The mobile dashboard shows the same data as the web app, optimized for smaller screens:

| Section | Access |
|---|---|
| **Today's Status** | Visible on the home screen immediately after check-in |
| **Attendance Records** | Tap **My Attendance** in the bottom navigation |
| **Performance Score** | Tap **Performance** in the bottom navigation |
| **Notifications** | Tap the bell icon (top right) |
| **Chatbot** | Tap the chat bubble icon (bottom right) |

---

## Push Notifications

When notifications are enabled, the app delivers alerts in the following scenarios:

| Notification | Trigger |
|---|---|
| Check-In Reminder | Sent at a configured time if no check-in is recorded |
| Anomaly Detected | Triggered when an anomaly is flagged on your account |
| Performance Score Change | Sent when your score moves by more than 10 points |
| System Announcement | Sent by your administrator for platform updates |

Tap any notification to open the relevant section of the app directly.

To manage notification preferences: **Profile → Settings → Notifications**.

---

## Offline Behavior

The mobile app requires an active internet connection for check-in, check-out, and data loading.

| Feature | Offline Available? |
|---|---|
| Check-In / Check-Out | ❌ No — requires live GPS and server validation |
| Viewing past attendance records | ✅ Yes — cached for last 7 days |
| Viewing performance score | ✅ Yes — cached from last sync |
| Chatbot | ❌ No — requires server connection |
| Notifications | ✅ Yes — delivered when connection resumes |

When connectivity is restored, the app syncs automatically. No manual action is required.

## App Updates

The app updates automatically through the Play Store or App Store when a new version is available. If your organization distributes the app internally, your IT team will push updates via MDM.

Always keep the app updated to ensure compatibility with the latest backend features and security patches.
