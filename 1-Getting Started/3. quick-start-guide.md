# Quick Start Guide

This guide walks you through everything you need to do before your first attendance check-in - from logging in for the first time to completing a GPS-validated session. The entire setup takes approximately 5–10 minutes.

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, confirm the following:

- [ ] Your account has been created by your administrator (you will receive a welcome email with your credentials).
- [ ] Your device meets the [System Requirements](./system-requirements.md).
- [ ] Camera and Location permissions are enabled on your device.
- [ ] You are connected to a network with at least 2 Mbps bandwidth.

---

## Step 1 - Log In

1. Open the web app in your browser or launch the mobile app.
2. Enter your **email address** and **temporary password** from the welcome email.
3. You will be prompted to set a new password on first login.
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - At least one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character
4. Click **Set Password** and proceed to the dashboard.

> If you do not receive a welcome email within 10 minutes of account creation, check your spam folder or contact your administrator.

---

## Step 2 - Complete Face Enrollment

Face enrollment is required before your first check-in. This registers your facial profile in the system for identity verification.

1. From the dashboard, click **Complete Face Enrollment** (you will also see a prompt banner if enrollment is pending).
2. Allow camera access when your browser or device prompts you.
3. Position your face within the on-screen frame - ensure:
   - Your face is well-lit (avoid strong backlighting)
   - You are looking directly at the camera
   - No obstructions (sunglasses, mask) are covering your face
4. The system will capture multiple frames automatically. Do not move until the capture bar reaches 100%.
5. A confirmation message - **Enrollment Successful** - will appear when complete.

**If enrollment fails:**
- Re-check lighting conditions and try again.
- After 3 failed attempts, contact your administrator to reset your enrollment.

---

## Step 3 - Validate Your GPS Location

GPS validation confirms that you are checking in from within an approved office zone.

1. When prompted, click **Allow Location Access** in your browser or device.
2. The system will display your detected location on a map with the office geofence boundary shown.
3. If your location falls **within** the boundary, you will see a green **Location Verified** indicator.
4. If your location shows **Outside Zone**, wait a few seconds and retry - GPS signals can take a moment to stabilize, especially indoors.

> If you are consistently flagged as outside the zone while physically present, contact your administrator. GPS accuracy can be affected by building structure or device hardware.

---

## Step 4 - Complete Your First Check-In

1. From the dashboard, click **Check In**.
2. The system will run face verification and GPS validation simultaneously.
3. Once both pass, your check-in is recorded and a confirmation appears with your login timestamp.
4. Your attendance status for the day updates to **Present** on your dashboard.

**To check out:**
1. Click **Check Out** when your workday ends.
2. Face verification runs again.
3. Your work hours for the day are calculated and displayed.

---

## Step 5 - Explore Your Dashboard

After your first check-in, your personal dashboard is active. Key areas to explore:

| Dashboard Section | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Attendance Summary** | Present / Absent / Late counts for the current month |
| **Work Hours** | Daily and weekly work hour totals |
| **Performance Score** | Your current score with a breakdown of contributing factors |
| **Notifications** | System alerts, anomaly flags, and reminders |
| **Chatbot** | Ask questions about your attendance data in plain language |

## Common First-Login Issues

| Issue | Likely Cause | Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| Welcome email not received | Email in spam, or wrong email on record | Check spam; contact administrator |
| Camera not detected | Browser permission denied | Enable camera in browser site settings |
| Face enrollment keeps failing | Poor lighting or camera quality | Improve lighting; try a different device if possible |
| GPS shows wrong location | Slow GPS lock or indoor interference | Wait 10–15 seconds and retry; move closer to a window |
| Check-in button is greyed out | Face enrollment not complete | Complete enrollment first (Step 2) |
| Password reset link expired | Link is valid for 24 hours only | Request a new link from your administrator |
